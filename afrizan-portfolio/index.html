About Us

At Afrizan, we aim to tackle the challenges faced by African artisans in showcasing and selling their handmade products. Our project provides a centralized e-commerce platform that connects artisans with a global audience, thereby fostering economic opportunities and empowering local craftsmanship. Who We Help/Users Our project benefits African artisans and craftsmen looking to expand their market reach. Our users include both sellers (artisans) and buyers interested in unique, handmade products.
